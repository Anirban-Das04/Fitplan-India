💡 Indian Nutrition Tips
🫙
Dal is Your Best Friend
1 cup cooked dal = 18g protein, 230 kcal. Masoor, moong, toor — rotate daily for complete amino acids. Best post-workout food.
🫓
Roti vs Rice — Know When
Roti (whole wheat) has more fibre and protein than rice. Eat roti at dinner. Rice before/after workouts for fast energy.
🥛
Curd Every Day
1 cup curd = 11g protein + probiotics + calcium. Eat post-meal for digestion. Homemade curd is 3x better than packaged.
🌿
Haldi + Jeera + Methi
Haldi reduces inflammation (anti-cortisol). Jeera boosts digestion. Methi controls blood sugar. Add to every meal.
🥜
Peanuts — Budget Protein
₹30 peanut butter = 25g protein. Best affordable protein source for Indian fitness goals. 2 tbsp = 200 kcal + 8g protein.
⏰
Eat on Indian Time
Breakfast: 7–9 AM · Lunch: 12–1 PM (biggest meal) · Dinner: 7–8 PM. Avoid eating after 9 PM — fat storage increases 40%.
